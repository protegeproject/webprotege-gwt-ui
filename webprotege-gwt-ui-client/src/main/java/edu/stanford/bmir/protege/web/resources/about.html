About WebProtégé

iCAT-X is a Web-based collaborative authoring tool used for editing the Foundation of the WHO Family of Classifications (WHO-FIC), including the International Classification of Diseases 11th Revision (ICD-11), the International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health (ICF), and the International Classification of Health Interventions (ICHI). iCAT-X is currently accessible to WHO’s internal editors. If you require access, please contact the WHO Classication Officer.

iCAT-X is based on the open-source tool WebProtégé, which is developed by the Protégé team in the Biomedical Informatics Research Group (BMIR) at Stanford University, California, USA.

The Stanford work is supported by Grant GM121724 from the National Institute of General Medical Sciences at the United States National Institutes of Health.
